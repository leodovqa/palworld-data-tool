Ultra Teams Guide (2026 Meta)

To defeat Bellanoir Libero Ultra in the 2026 meta (v0.7 and beyond), you need to account for her massive HP pool (around 1.2 million) and her unique two-phase typing.

She starts as Dark type but transforms into Ice type when she reaches her final HP threshold (roughly the last 15–20%). This makes standard Dragon teams dangerous because she will shred them in the final phase.

The "Ultra" Team Composition

Since raid bosses now target the player more aggressively, your party should be built around Player Damage Enhancement while your base Pals provide the heavy lifting.

1. The Party (Your Personal Loadout)

  • Mount 1 (Phase 1): Chillet / Chillet Ignis. Riding Chillet converts your player damage to Dragon, which is super effective against her Dark phase.
  • Mount 2 (Phase 2): Ragnahawk / Pyrin. When she turns to Ice, switch to these to convert your damage to Fire.
  • Support: 4x Gobfin (fully condensed). Their "Angry Shark" skill stacks, giving you a massive boost to your own attack power.
  • Passives for Party: Aim for Vanguard and Stronghold Strategist on every Gobfin and mount to buff your own stats.

2. The Base Army (The 15–20 Pals at your Altar)

You need to a distinct groups. Use the Palbox to swap them quickly when the phase changes.

Phase Recommended Pals Reason
Phase 1 (Dark) Jetragon / Astegon Jetragon provides the highest Dragon DPS. Astegon (Dark/Dragon) has the best natural resistance to her Dark beams.
Phase 2 (Ice) Jormuntide Ignis / Blazamut Ryu Jormuntide Ignis is the "MVP" because its Fire/Dragon typing resists her attacks in both phases. Blazamut Ryu is the current DPS king for Fire damage.
All-Rounder Selyne If you don't want to swap, Selyne is the most versatile post-v0.7 Pal for raids due to its high burst magic damage.

Optimal Passive Skills (2026 Meta)

For your Base Pals, move away from old skills like Ferocious. You want the "Big Four" for maximum boss DPS:

  • Legend: (Atk +20%, Def +20%, Move Speed +15%)
  • Demon God: (Atk +30%, Def +5%) — Superior to Ferocious; found on raid-tier Pals.
  • Musclehead: (Atk +30%) — Essential raw damage.
  • Serenity: (Atk +10%, Cooldown -30%) — Crucial. This allows your Pals to spam high-damage moves like Holy Burst or Dragon Meteor much more frequently.

Pro Strategy: The "Three Pillars" of Success

  1. The Food Buff: Feed your base Pals Mozzarina Cheeseburgers before summoning. It grants a 20% attack boost and, more importantly, keeps their hunger from dropping, which prevents a 20% stat penalty mid-fight.
  2. Skill Selection: Ensure your Pals have at least one low-cooldown move (2–4 seconds). If they only have high-power/high-cooldown moves, they will stand idle for 10 seconds after one attack, losing massive DPS.
  3. Base Safety: Build your Summoning Altar on a raised stone platform or in a base with no critical infrastructure. Bellanoir's "Nightmare Ray" will delete chests and crafting stations instantly.
